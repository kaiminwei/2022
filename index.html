Kaimin Wei


I am an Associate Professor in the College of Information Science and Technology at Jinan University. I received my Ph.D. from Beihang University. I was selected as the Young Pearl River Scholar. The easiest way to contact me is through email at weikaimin (at) gmail.com.

My main research interest are computer network and artificial intelligence, with a particular emphasis on security techniques and algorithm optimization in mobile crowdsensing and AI.
